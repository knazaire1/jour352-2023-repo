10. Why do we use media queries?

we use media queries to edit how a webpage displays depending on the size of the screen
in which the elements are being viewed. For example, specifying the rule that boxes
should stack when the viewport is narrower instead of being side by side.

11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

however san-serif fonts don't have the legs and convey a more light and modern style.
